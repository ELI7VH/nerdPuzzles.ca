What we are doing is learning programming with the language, Python! The goal of this community is that within a year, you will learn how to program to a point where all your wildest ideas will be possible! even things like this website!

The one cool quirk about this club is that the entire class does not progress until everybody does the weekly challenge! Nobody gets left behind!
